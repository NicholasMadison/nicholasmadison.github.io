What does SOS offer?

Our trained and licensed therapists integrate Hippotherapy into our patients' plan of care, along with traditional therapy tools and strategies. The term Hippotherapy, refers to how therapy professionals use evidence-based practice and clinical reasoning in the purposeful manipulation of equine movement. This serves as a therapy tool to engage sensory, neuromotor, and cognitive systems to promote functional outcomes.
